    <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
    <p class="last-updated">Last Updated: November 25, 2025</p>
  </div>

  <div class="privacy-content-wrapper">
    <div class="privacy-main-content">
      <div class="privacy-intro">
        <p>
          This policy explains how SIO Abul Fazal Area ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects
          personal data for the Muqawama Football Tournament. It follows the structure and safeguards used by other
          grassroots tournaments while remaining tailored to our community event.
        </p>
        <p>
          By using or accessing our Sites and Services in any manner, you accept the practices and policies outlined in this Privacy Notice and you acknowledge that we may process and share your information.
        </p>
      </div>

      <section id="information-we-collect" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>Information We Collect</h2>
    <div class="info-grid">
      <div class="info-card">
        <h4>Contact & Profile Data</h4>
        <p>Player and team representative names, phone numbers, email addresses, jersey preferences, and category selections submitted during registration or queries.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="info-card">
        <h4>Team & Performance Data</h4>
        <p>Team names, squad lists, match results, individual awards, and multimedia captured during the tournament.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="info-card">
        <h4>Technical Data</h4>
        <p>Session timestamps, and aggregate analytics collected via our website hosting stack.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

      <section id="how-we-use" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>How We Use Your Data</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Process registrations, payments, and roster confirmations.</li>
      <li>Share fixtures, updates, and emergency alerts with team contacts.</li>
      <li>Publish tables, results, media recaps, and award announcements.</li>
      <li>Improve tournament logistics by analyzing attendance and engagement statistics.</li>
      <li>Comply with safety obligations, venue rules, and lawful requests.</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="info-note">
      We rely on consent (when you submit a form), legitimate interest (to run the competition smoothly), and
      contract fulfillment (providing match slots and services) as our primary legal bases.
    </p>
  </section>

      <section id="ai-image-processing" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>Enhanced Player Images</h2>
    <p>
      As part of our tournament experience, we may use artificial intelligence (AI) tools to enhance and transform 
      player photographs into professional-looking images for player cards, promotional materials, and tournament displays.
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Your submitted photos may be processed through AI image enhancement tools to create stylized, professional player portraits.</li>
      <li>These AI-generated images may be used on our website, social media, printed materials, and tournament displays.</li>
      <li>Original photos are retained and AI-processed versions are created as additional assets.</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="info-note">
      <strong>By registering for the tournament, you consent to having your photographs processed using AI enhancement tools 
      and agree that the resulting images may be used for tournament-related purposes.</strong> If you do not wish to have 
      your images AI-enhanced, please contact us at <a href="mailto:muqawamah@sio-abulfazal.org">muqawamah@sio-abulfazal.org</a> before or during registration.
    </p>
  </section>

      <section id="sharing-disclosure" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>Sharing & Disclosure</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Operational vendors (payment gateways, design/printing partners, volunteer staff) receive only the data required to serve their function.</li>
      <li>Media and community partners may access match footage or photos for promotional coverage.</li>
      <li>We will disclose data if required by law, safety directives, or to investigate misconduct.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>We do <strong>not</strong> sell personal data or allow external marketing without opt-in permission.</p>
  </section>

      <section id="storage-security" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>Storage, Retention & Security</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Digital records are stored on secure, access-controlled services aligned with Supabase infrastructure standards.</li>
      <li>We retain registration and results data for up to 90 days to help teams enroll and to publish historical statistics, unless we are legally required to keep them longer.</li>
      <li>Media assets may live longer online as part of our community archive. You can request removal if a photo or clip features you.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

      <section id="your-rights" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>Your Rights</h2>
    <p>Participants can request to:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Access, correct, or delete their personal data.</li>
      <li>Withdraw consent for marketing communications.</li>
      <li>Limit how we profile or publish their imagery.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>Send requests to <a href="mailto:muqawamah@sio-abulfazal.org">muqawamah@sio-abulfazal.org</a>. We respond within 30 minutes.</p>
  </section>
