        </p>

      <section id="eligibility" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>1. Eligibility</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>Only amateur footballers may participate. Professional club-affiliated players are prohibited. Violations lead to disqualification without refund.</li>
          <li>Teams must register 7â€“11 players (7 starters + up to 4 substitutes) before the published deadline.</li>
          <li>Players must match the category (Open/U-17/others) they select during registration and carry valid photo ID at the venue.</li>
          <li>Squads are locked once fixtures are released. No inter-team transfers or additions between matches.</li>
        </ul>
      </section>

      <section id="registration-fees" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>2. Registration & Fees</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>Entries are confirmed only after we receive the full fee and roster details.</li>
          <li>Fees are non-refundable except where stated in the <a href="/muqawamah/refund-policy/">Refund Policy</a>.</li>
          <li>The organizer may reject or wait-list teams to balance categories or ensure fair scheduling.</li>
        </ul>
      </section>

      <section id="match-rules" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>3. Match Rules</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>Format: FIFA-inspired 7-a-side rules, 10 minutes per half, 2-minute interval.</li>
          <li>Kick-off punctuality matters. Teams arriving more than 2 minutes late forfeit the fixture.</li>
          <li>Referees and match officials have full authority on-field. Decisions are final and not subject to protest.</li>
          <li>Teams manage their own jerseys (contrasting colors), shin guards, and hydration.</li>
          <li>Group-stage guarantees at least two matches per team, followed by knockout rounds.</li>
        </ul>
      </section>

      <section id="conduct" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>4. Conduct & Fair Play</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>Abuse, discrimination, or violent conduct (players or spectators) results in immediate removal.</li>
          <li>Using ineligible, suspended, or impersonated players triggers disqualification and forfeiture of prizes.</li>
          <li>Teams are responsible for the behavior of their supporters within the venue premises.</li>
        </ul>
      </section>

      <section id="awards-media" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>5. Awards & Media</h2>
        <p>
          Champions and runners-up receive awards. Individual awards include Top Goal Scorer, Top Goalkeeper,
          and Best Player. By entering, participants grant us the right to capture and share photos/videos for recap content,
          highlights, and sponsor deliverables. Contact us if you need a specific asset removed.
        </p>
      </section>

      <section id="liability" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>6. Liability</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>All players participate at their own risk. Teams are encouraged to carry personal medical insurance.</li>
          <li>The organizer is not liable for loss of property, personal injury, or transportation issues unless caused by gross negligence.</li>
          <li>Medical support is available on-site for first response only.</li>
