    <h1>Refund & Cancellation Policy</h1>
    <p class="last-updated">Last Updated: November 25, 2025</p>
  </div>

  <div class="privacy-content-wrapper">
    <div class="privacy-main-content">
      <div class="privacy-intro">
        <p>
          Registrations keep the Muqawama Football Tournament community-driven and logistically sound. This policy clarifies
          when fees are retained, when credits are issued, and how we handle unforeseen circumstances.
        </p>
      </div>

      <section id="general-rule" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>General Rule</h2>
        <p>
          All entry fees are <strong>non-refundable</strong> once a team secures its slot. This helps us secure referees, medical staff, merchandise, and media resources for every participating squad.
        </p>
      </section>

      <section id="organizer-changes" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>Organizer-Initiated Changes</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>If we cancel the entire tournament, teams may choose between a 100% refund or automatic transfer to the next edition.</li>
          <li>If a category is merged, affected teams can accept the new slot or request a credit that remains valid for 1 month.</li>
        </ul>
      </section>

      <section id="team-cancellations" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>Team Cancellations</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>Withdrawals after payment are not eligible for cash refunds.</li>
          <li>Teams may nominate a replacement squad before fixtures are published. The replacement must complete the waiver process and honor all rules.</li>
          <li>No-shows or late arrivals (over 3 minutes after kick-off) count as forfeits. Fees will not be refunded or transferred.</li>
        </ul>
      </section>

      <section id="force-majeure" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>Force Majeure</h2>
        <p>
          Severe weather, public safety directives, or venue closures may force us to postpone matches. We will prioritize
          rescheduling within the same season.
        </p>
      </section>

      <section id="request-support" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>How to Request Support</h2>
        <p>Send the following to <a href="mailto:info@sio-abulfazal.org">info@sio-abulfazal.org</a>:</p>
        <ul>
          <li>Team name and captain contact</li>
          <li>Payment reference or UTR number</li>
          <li>Reason for the request and any supporting documents</li>
        </ul>
        <p>We review submissions within 30 minutes and communicate next steps via email.</p>
      </section>

      <section id="contact" class="privacy-section">
        <h2>Need Assistance?</h2>
        <p>For any refund-related inquiries, please contact us:</p>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:info@sio-abulfazal.org">info@sio-abulfazal.org</a></li>

        </ul>
